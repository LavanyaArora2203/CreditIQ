ABC Finance Ltd. - Personal Loan Policy (Sample)

This is a fictional policy document created for demonstration and
testing purposes.

Eligibility

-   Applicant age: 21–60 years at loan application.
-   Indian resident with valid KYC.
-   Salaried applicants require at least 12 months of continuous
    employment.
-   Self-employed applicants require at least 24 months of business
    continuity.
-   Minimum monthly income:
    -   Salaried: INR 25,000
    -   Self-employed: INR 40,000

Credit Policy

-   Preferred credit score: 750+
-   700–749: Eligible subject to underwriting review.
-   650–699: Higher interest rate or reduced loan amount.
-   Below 650: Normally declined unless exceptional compensating factors
    exist.

Loan Amount

-   Minimum: INR 50,000
-   Maximum: INR 2,000,000
-   Maximum amount shall not exceed 20 times the applicant’s verified
    monthly income.

Tenure

-   Minimum: 12 months
-   Maximum: 60 months

Debt-to-Income (DTI)

-   Existing EMI + Proposed EMI should generally not exceed 40% of
    verified monthly income.
-   Applications above 40% DTI require manual approval.
-   Applications above 50% DTI are rejected.

Interest Rates

-   Base interest rate: 10.50% p.a.
-   Final rate depends on credit score, employment profile, income
    stability, and internal risk grade.
-   Interest may range from 10.50% to 18.00%.

Processing Fee

-   Up to 2% of the sanctioned amount.
-   GST applicable as per prevailing regulations.

Required Documents

-   Aadhaar Card
-   PAN Card
-   Address Proof
-   Last 3 Salary Slips (or equivalent income proof)
-   Last 6 Months Bank Statements
-   Passport-size Photograph

Verification

-   KYC verification is mandatory.
-   AML and sanctions screening are mandatory.
-   Income verification must be successful before disbursement.

Approval Conditions

-   Underwriting may reduce the sanctioned amount based on
    affordability.
-   Interest rate may be revised based on final risk assessment.
-   Approval validity: 15 calendar days.

Disbursement

-   Processing fee and applicable charges are deducted before
    disbursement.
-   Funds are transferred only after successful verification and
    acceptance of the sanction letter.

Rejection Criteria

-   Fraudulent documents.
-   Failed KYC or AML checks.
-   Credit score below policy threshold.
-   Unstable employment or unverifiable income.
-   Excessive existing debt obligations.
